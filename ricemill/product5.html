Nellore Rice

About Nellore Rice

Nellore rice is a renowned variety of rice that originates from the Nellore district in the neighboring state of Andhra Pradesh, but it is also widely cultivated and consumed in Telangana.

Characteristics:

  • Grain Type: Nellore rice typically consists of medium to long grains, which are known for their fine texture and quality.
  • Aroma and Flavor: It has a mild, pleasant aroma and a slightly sweet flavor, making it a favorite for various dishes.
  • Appearance: The grains are slender and may have a glossy finish, contributing to an appealing look when cooked.

Usage:

  • Daily Consumption
  • Special Dishes
  • Traditional Recipes
