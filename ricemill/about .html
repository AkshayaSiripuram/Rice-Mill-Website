About Sri Lalitha Rice Mill

Discover our legacy and commitment

Our Legacy

Sri Lalitha Rice Mill has a rich heritage spanning over three decades. Our journey began in 1985 with a passion for delivering the finest quality rice to our customers. Since then, we have evolved into a leading rice mill in the region, renowned for our dedication to excellence.

Our Commitment

At Sri Lalitha Rice Mill, we are committed to upholding the highest standards of quality, sustainability, and customer satisfaction. Our team works tirelessly to ensure that every grain of rice that leaves our mill meets our stringent quality criteria.

Our Impact

Beyond our business objectives, we strive to make a positive impact on the communities we serve and the environment. Through responsible sourcing, sustainable practices, and community initiatives, we aim to create a brighter future for generations to come.

Our Future

As we look ahead, Sri Lalitha Rice Mill is committed to continuous innovation and improvement. We will continue to invest in technology, research, and development to ensure that we remain at the forefront of the rice industry, delivering unparalleled quality and value to our customers.
