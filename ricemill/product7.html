HMT Rice

About HMT Rice

HMT rice is a popular variety of rice in Telangana and neighboring regions, known for its quality and culinary versatility.

Characteristics:

  • Grain Type: HMT rice is a medium-grain variety. The grains are slender and slightly shorter than long-grain varieties like Basmati.
  • Texture: It has a firm texture when cooked, which makes it suitable for various types of dishes. The grains remain separate and do not become overly sticky.
  • Aroma and Flavor: HMT rice has a mild aroma and a subtle flavor, making it an excellent choice for a wide range of recipes.

Usage:

  • Everyday Cooking: Due to its versatile nature, HMT rice is widely used for everyday meals. It pairs well with curries, dals, and vegetable dishes.
  • Special Dishes: It's suitable for making special dishes like biryani, pulao, and fried rice, where the texture and appearance of the rice are important.
  • Traditional Recipes: HMT rice is also used in traditional South Indian dishes, such as tamarind rice, lemon rice, and curd rice.
