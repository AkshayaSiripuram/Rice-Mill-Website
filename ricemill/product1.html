About Sona Rice

Sona Rice is a medium grain raw rice sourced from the local villages of Andhra Pradesh. It is aged up to 6 months before packaging, giving it a rich and unique aroma. The golden brown rice is rich in nutrients and minerals, and expert care has been taken to ensure its quality. It is perfect for making traditional dishes and can be a staple in your pantry.

Additional Information:

  • Origin: Andhra Pradesh, India
  • Aging: 6 months
  • Nutritional Value: High in nutrients and minerals
  • Best for: Traditional dishes
