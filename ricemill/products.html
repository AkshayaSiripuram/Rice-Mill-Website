Sri Seetha Rama Paddy Rice Mill - Our Products

Sona Masoori Rice

Medium grain raw rice sourced from local villages of Andhra Pradesh. Aged up to 6 months before packaging with a rich and unique aroma.

HMT Rice

This is a high-quality, medium-grain rice variety that is popular in Telangana. It is known for its taste and texture, making it suitable for a variety of dishes.

Brown Rice

Whole grain rice, rich in magnesium, phosphorus, and antioxidants. Ideal for a healthy diet.

Basmati Rice

Long grain aromatic rice traditionally from the Indian subcontinent. Known for its distinct fragrance and flavor.

Idly Rice

A variety specifically used for making idlis, a popular South Indian dish. This rice has a high starch content, which helps in fermenting the idli batter effectively.

Nellore Rice

Originating from the Nellore district, this rice variety is known for its fine grains and superior taste. It's often used for special occasions and traditional dishes.

Black Rice

This is a less common but highly nutritious variety of rice that is grown in smaller quantities. It is rich in antioxidants and has a unique flavor.
